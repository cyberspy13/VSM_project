Project Specification: Vehicle Service Management (VSM) Extension
1. Overview
The objective is to allow a business to track vehicles owned by their customers and record service histories. When a mechanic or service advisor posts a Sales Invoice for service work, the system should automatically log the service history against the specific vehicle.

2. Data Model (Tables)
You will need to create two new custom tables and extend one standard table.

Table 1: Vehicle

Vehicle No. (Code[20], Primary Key, mapped to a No. Series)
Customer No. (Code[20], TableRelation to Customer)
Make (Text[50] — e.g., Toyota, Honda, Ford, Volvo, Suzuki)
Model (Text[50])
Registration No. (Code[20])
Next Service Date (Date)

Table 2: Vehicle Service Ledger Entry

Entry No. (Integer, Primary Key, AutoIncrement)
Vehicle No. (Code[20], TableRelation to Vehicle)
Service Date (Date)
Service Description (Text[100])
Posted Document No. (Code[20])

Table Extension 3: Sales Header & Sales Invoice Header
Add a new field: Vehicle No. (Code[20], TableRelation to Vehicle). This field should only show vehicles belonging to the selected Sell-to Customer No.

3. User Interface (Pages)

Vehicle Card & List: Standard data entry pages for the Vehicle table.

Customer Card Extension: Add an action in the ribbon to open the Vehicle List filtered to that specific customer.

Sales Order/Invoice Extension: Add the Vehicle No. field to the General FastTab of the Sales Invoice and Sales Order pages.

4. Business Logic (Codeunits & Event Subscribers)
This is where the mid-level complexity comes in. You need to automate the creation of the Service Ledger Entry without modifying base Microsoft code.
NEED to be doing
Event Subscriber 1 (Data Transfer): Subscribe to the event that transfers fields from the Sales Header to the Sales Invoice Header during the posting routine so the Vehicle No. is retained on the posted document.

Event Subscriber 2 (Ledger Creation): Subscribe to the OnAfterPostSalesDoc (or equivalent Codeunit 80 event).

Condition: If the posted document has a Vehicle No. assigned, the code should automatically insert a new record into the Vehicle Service Ledger Entry table.

Mapping: Map the Posting Date to the Service Date, and grab the first text line of the invoice for the Service Description.

Codeunit (Date Calculation): Create a procedure that recalculates the Next Service Date on the Vehicle Card (e.g., adding exactly 12 months to the latest Service Date) and updates the Vehicle record.
